## J! Trainer

J! Trainer is a Jeopardy! training application. The goal is to help users practice their Jeopardy! knowledge using real clues from the show, with tracking of performance statistics over time.

Players can participate in drills, in which they are served a set of clues one at a time. They may attempt a response or pass. Incorrect responses decrease the player's coryat score by the clue value; correct responses do the opposite. Passes do not affect a player's coryat score.

Players may end drills at any time.
